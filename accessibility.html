Accessibility

Accessibility & AODA Statement

Toriven Health is committed to providing an accessible digital experience for all users, including persons with disabilities, in accordance with Ontario's accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

Toriven Health (Toriven Health Inc.) is committed to ensuring equal access to our website and digital services for all individuals, including persons with disabilities. We are working to meet and maintain compliance with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005 (AODA) and the Integrated Accessibility Standards Regulation (IASR) under Ontario Regulation 191/11.

Our target standard for web accessibility is Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 Level AA, as required under the IASR for information and communications standards. We are additionally building toward WCAG 2.1 Level AA as best practice beyond the legal minimum.

Toriven Health is a pre-launch organization. Our website is currently in development. We are building with accessibility as a design requirement, not an afterthought, and will conduct a formal accessibility audit prior to clinical launch.

Applicable Standards

This website is being developed with reference to the following standards and legislation:

  • Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005 (AODA)
  • Integrated Accessibility Standards Regulation (O. Reg. 191/11) — Information and Communications Standards, Customer Service Standards
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0, Level AA — published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
  • Ontario Human Rights Code — with respect to equal treatment in services

Current Status

Toriven Health has not yet launched clinical services. During the pre-launch period, our website is informational only. We are actively working to ensure the following accessibility features are in place at launch:

  • Semantic HTML structure with appropriate heading hierarchy
  • Sufficient colour contrast ratios meeting WCAG 2.0 Level AA (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
  • Descriptive link text that is meaningful out of context
  • Alt text for all informational images
  • Keyboard navigability for all interactive elements
  • No content that relies solely on colour to convey meaning
  • Consistent navigation structure across all pages
  • Resizable text support without loss of content or functionality

Known Limitations

As a pre-launch website under active development, some pages may not yet fully meet WCAG 2.0 Level AA. We are committed to identifying and resolving these gaps before and after our clinical launch. A formal third-party accessibility audit is planned prior to opening.

Multi-Year Accessibility Plan

In accordance with the IASR, Toriven Health is committed to developing and maintaining a multi-year accessibility plan. Our current commitments include:

  • Pre-launch (2026): Internal accessibility review of all patient-facing web pages against WCAG 2.0 AA criteria
  • At launch (2026): Publish updated accessibility statement with formal audit results; ensure all new content published after launch meets WCAG 2.0 AA
  • Post-launch (ongoing): Annual review of website accessibility; address reported barriers within a reasonable timeframe; train staff on accessible content creation

Accessible Formats and Communication Supports

Upon request, Toriven Health will make reasonable efforts to provide information in accessible formats or with appropriate communication supports. Requests can be submitted using the contact information below. We will respond to accessibility format requests in a timely manner and at no additional charge.

Why Toriven Health

A place where clinicians thrive.

We built the clinical environment we'd want to work in ourselves — flexible, collaborative, and genuinely committed to doing things right.

💻

Fully Virtual Practice

See clients from anywhere in Ontario. No commute, no office politics — just focused clinical work from a space that works for you.

🤝

Real Multidisciplinary Collaboration

Consult with psychologists, SLPs, OTs, social workers, and BCBAs within a shared clinical framework — not in isolation. Your colleagues are a message away.

📋

Admin Done For You

Scheduling, billing, intake, and documentation systems are handled by our admin team so you can focus on what you trained to do: clinical work.

🧠

Neurodiversity-Affirming Culture

Professional Development

Access to peer consultation, clinical supervision, CPD resources, and a growing community of regulated allied health professionals.

Caseload You're Proud Of

Our Culture

We practice what
we preach.

The values we bring to client care are the same ones we apply internally. Transparent. Collaborative. Genuinely human.

Scope of Practice, Always

You will never be pressured to see a client outside your training. We match caseloads to competency — every time.

Honest Communication

Feedback goes both ways. We expect our clinical team to tell us what's working and what isn't — and we actually act on it.

No Productivity Theatre

We care about client outcomes, not session counts. There are no arbitrary quotas or pressure to see more clients than is clinically appropriate.

Clinician Wellbeing Matters

Burnout is real. We build realistic caseloads, offer peer consultation, and respect that you have a life outside of your practice.

"I joined because I was tired of the fragmented system and wanted to actually collaborate with other clinicians. The referral pathway here is genuine — I regularly consult with the OT and SLP on shared cases and it makes a real difference for families."
— Founding clinical advisor

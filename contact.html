Important Notices

Please read before reaching out.

These notices exist to protect you and to meet our obligations under Ontario's regulatory and privacy framework.

🚫

We Are Not Yet Open — No Clinical Services Are Available

Toriven Health is a pre-launch organization. We are not currently open to clients, patients, or referrals. No clinical assessments, therapy, consultations, or any other professional health services are available at this time. Our anticipated launch is late spring or summer 2026, subject to change. Do not attempt to seek clinical care through this website or through our social media channels.

📋

This Website Is for Informational Purposes Only

The content on this website — including all descriptions of services, programs, clinical approaches, and team information — is provided for general informational purposes only. Nothing on this website constitutes medical advice, clinical guidance, a diagnosis, a treatment recommendation, or a substitute for consultation with a regulated health professional. This website is not a clinical tool and should not be used as one. If you require health information or care, please consult a qualified, regulated health professional in your area.

⚖️

No Therapeutic or Clinical Relationship Is Established

Visiting this website, browsing our content, following us on social media, sending us a message, or contacting us by any means does not establish a therapeutic relationship, a clinician–patient relationship, or any professional relationship of any kind between you and Toriven Health or any of its clinicians. A therapeutic or clinical relationship with Toriven Health can only be established after: formal intake has been completed, a clinical match has been accepted in writing, and all required consent documentation has been signed by the relevant parties. Until those steps are complete, no clinical obligation exists.

🔒

Privacy & Personal Information

At this stage, Toriven Health does not collect personal health information through this website. Please do not send personal health information — including details about diagnoses, symptoms, medications, or clinical history — through any website contact mechanism, social media message, or general email. If you do choose to send us a general message via social media or any channel we may make available, be aware that those channels are not PHIPA-designated secure systems and are not appropriate for personal health information. Our PHIPA-compliant clinical intake systems will be available at launch.

🆘

If You Are in Crisis — Please Do Not Contact Us

Toriven Health is not a crisis service and we are not yet operational. We cannot provide any form of emergency support, crisis counselling, or urgent clinical response. If you or someone you know is in immediate distress or danger, please contact:

📞
9-8-8 — Canada Suicide Crisis Helpline
Call or text 9-8-8 · Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, across Canada
🏥
Your Nearest Emergency Department
Go to your nearest hospital emergency department for immediate mental health or medical crisis support

What to Expect at Launch

What Toriven Health will offer.

When we open in 2026, here is what families and individuals across Ontario will be able to access.

🧠

Psychological Assessments

ADHD, autism, psychoeducational, and neuropsychological assessments for children, teens, and adults.

💬

Therapy & Counselling

CBT, trauma-focused therapy, DBT-informed, and more — for individuals and families across all ages.

🗣️

Speech-Language Pathology

Assessment and therapy for language, articulation, fluency, literacy, feeding, and communication.

🤲

Occupational Therapy

Sensory processing, fine motor, daily living skills, and school participation for children and teens.

📋

Executive Function Programs

Group and individual programs supporting planning, organization, and independent learning.

🏫

School & Academic Support

IEP navigation, dyslexia remediation, homework support, and post-secondary transition planning.

🌸

Women's Mental Health

Specialized support for perinatal mental health, trauma, hormonal mood shifts, and life transitions.

💼

Workplace & Life Transitions

Return-to-work planning, communication confidence, and executive functioning in the workplace.

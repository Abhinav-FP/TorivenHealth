Our Story

Built to close a gap Ontario families know firsthand.

At Toriven Health, we set out to solve a problem too many Ontario families know firsthand: excellent mental health and developmental care that is fragmented, delayed, or simply out of reach for those who need it most.

We built a platform where regulated clinicians across psychology, therapy, SLP, OT, and behavioural science work together — creating coordinated support rather than a collection of disconnected appointments.

This is not fragmented care stitched together. It is a coordinated ecosystem — built for the diverse rhythms of modern Ontario families, and guided by a single purpose: faster access to exceptional clinical support for every Ontarian, without compromise on quality.

Virtual-First Care

Why virtual care works.

Virtual-first is not a compromise — it is an intentional design decision that expands access, improves consistency, and lets clinicians meet clients in the environments where life actually happens.

🏥

In-Person When Clinically Determined

Virtual-first is the standard. Where direct observation or standardized testing requires it, your clinician will determine if in-person is needed — explained clearly before you book, with no surprises.

🌐

No geography, no waitlists

Whether you're in Toronto, Northern Ontario, or a rural community, you access the same expert clinical team — without travel, without delays.

📅

Flexible scheduling that fits life

Work, school, family schedules — virtual care bends around your life, not the other way around. Evenings and flexible windows available.

🔗

Seamless multidisciplinary collaboration

When your OT, psychologist, and social worker are all on one platform, care coordination happens naturally — not through faxes and referrals.

🔒

PHIPA-compliant secure sessions

Designated virtual platforms are encrypted and PHIPA-compliant — protecting your personal health information at every touchpoint.

📋

Written summaries you can act on

Families and individuals receive practical written recommendations shareable with schools, physicians, and other care providers.

Mostly Virtual
Interviews, history-gathering, rating scales, and feedback sessions are conducted virtually across Ontario — no travel required for these components.
In-Person When Clinically Needed
Direct observation and certain standardized testing may require an in-person session at a clinical location. Your clinician determines this based on individual need — never a blanket requirement.
Always Discussed Before You Book
Format requirements are explained before you pay or commit. If in-person attendance is recommended, your clinician will tell you where, when, and why — with no surprises.
You will never be asked to attend in person without a clear clinical reason. All format decisions are made by your regulated clinician in accordance with their Ontario college standards and your individual needs.

The Founding Story

Two Decades Improving Care Systems. Built to Close the Gap.

Across Iowa, Australia, Kingston, and Hamilton, Amit worked alongside dedicated clinicians, physicians, and health system leaders who were genuinely trying to deliver excellent care. And yet, in every system, the same challenge kept appearing: families coordinating care across multiple clinicians without a shared record connecting them, parents repeating their child’s story to every new provider, and geography quietly determining the quality of care a family could access.

TorivenHealth™ was created to address exactly that — by bringing coordinated, multidisciplinary care together through a virtual-first model designed to make expert support accessible across Ontario.

One patient. One record. One coordinated team. That’s the founding principle of TorivenHealth™ — and it informs every clinical, operational, and technology decision we make.

Privacy Policy AODA Accessibility

🚫 Not Yet Open: Toriven Health is not yet open and is not currently accepting clients, patients, or referrals. Anticipated launch is late spring or summer 2026, subject to change.

📋 Informational Only: This website is for general informational purposes only. Nothing on this website constitutes medical advice, clinical guidance, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a regulated health professional for health concerns.

🔒 PHIPA: PHIPA-compliant secure systems will be used for clinical communication and patient records at launch. Do not send personal health information through social media or general web channels.

💳 Private Pay: Services will be private pay. Not covered by OHIP. Insurance reimbursement depends on individual plans. Clients are responsible for confirming insurance coverage. Receipts are provided for reimbursement.

⚖️ No Therapeutic Relationship: Visiting this website, contacting us, or following us on social media does not establish a therapeutic, clinical, or professional relationship of any kind. A therapeutic relationship is only formed after formal intake, clinical acceptance, and completion of required consent documentation.

🆘 Crisis Support: Toriven Health is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate distress, contact 9-8-8 (Canada Suicide Crisis Helpline) or your nearest emergency department.

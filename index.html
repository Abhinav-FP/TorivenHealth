About Toriven Health

We Believe Access to Expert Care Is Not a Privilege

Toriven Health was built on a simple idea: no family should have to travel hours, sit on a two-year waitlist, or navigate a fragmented system just to get the right support.

We are a virtual-first, multidisciplinary allied health team serving all of Ontario. Our clinicians — psychologists, social workers, psychotherapists, SLPs, and OTs — operate within their regulated college standards, follow evidence-based practice, and communicate with one another behind the scenes.

Our clinicians are registered with their respective Ontario regulatory colleges and follow evidence-based clinical practice standards.

"We take a neurodiversity-affirming, whole-person approach. We don't just assess and discharge — we walk alongside you."

Whether you're a parent seeking answers for your child, an adult navigating a late diagnosis, or a family that just needs a team that finally gets it — you're in the right place.

How It Works

Care That Fits Your Life

We are virtual-first — all care begins and is primarily delivered online, securely and comfortably, on your schedule. In-person assessment is only offered when your clinician determines it is clinically necessary.

Long wait times for specialized assessments and therapy remain a challenge across Ontario. Our virtual-first model helps reduce geographic barriers and improve access to regulated clinicians.

🌍

Accessible From Anywhere in Ontario

Whether you're in Toronto or Timmins, the same expert team is available to you — no geography barriers.

📅

Flexible, Family-Friendly Scheduling

Evening and weekend appointments available. We fit around school hours, nap times, and work schedules.

🔒

PHIPA-Compliant & Fully Encrypted

Your health information is protected under Ontario's Personal Health Information Protection Act — zero compromise on privacy.

🏥

In-Person When Clinically Determined

Virtual-first is the standard. Where your clinician determines in-person attendance is clinically necessary, this is communicated clearly before you book — never assumed.

Mostly Virtual
Interviews, history-gathering, rating scales, and feedback sessions are conducted virtually across Ontario — no travel required.
In-Person When Clinically Needed
Direct observation or standardized testing may require an in-person session at a clinical location — determined by your clinician, never assumed.
Always Discussed Before You Book
Format is explained before you pay or commit. Your clinician tells you where, when, and why — with no surprises.
You will never be asked to attend in person without a clear clinical reason. All format decisions are made by your regulated clinician in accordance with their Ontario college standards.
